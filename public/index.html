Welcome!

haneshi-web

Hi everyone, I'm Haneshi. I've always had this vague dream of becoming a developer, and I was fortunate enough to get the opportunity to study programming. So far, I've learned languages like Java, Python, and frameworks like Spring, along with HTML, scripts, and various libraries. As I continued my studies in web development, I always wanted to create my own personal web page, and now I’ve finally made it happen! I drew inspiration from the designs of the BBC in the UK and The New York Times, and I aimed to give it the feel of a web app made abroad. It’s nothing too fancy, but if you find anything awkward or off in terms of user experience (UX), flow, or design, I’d love to hear your feedback! It’s always welcome! If you'd like to check out my code, just click the button below to visit my GitHub.

I was born in Daejeon, South Korea 🗺️, and have lived in several cities throughout my life. 🌆 Currently, I reside in Hanam 🏡. I started breakdancing in high school and kept it up for about 10 years, 💃 but due to some personal circumstances and health issues, I had to stop. 😔

After that, I started learning graphic design 🎨, and it naturally led me to develop an interest in development, coding, and web programming. 💻 While studying programming, I discovered Stack Overflow 🧑‍💻, and I was captivated by how people from all over the world communicate and work together to solve each other's coding errors and challenges. 🌍🤝
